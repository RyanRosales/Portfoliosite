eSports Magazine

A handcrafted magazine that highlights the competitive gaming culture.

Overview

A design challenge I took on during spring 2018 that required original design and content, along with handcrafting the final product. In addition, I was given the freedom to choose any topic I wanted to cover. As an avid gamer, I wanted to cover the trending eSports culture and highlight the environment gamers live in.

During the course of this project, I was still grasping the fundamentals of design and this magazine pushed my creativity to its limits.

Layout

During the process of designing the layout of the magazine, I struggled because I had no prior knowledge of magazine design. As I kept working on the layout and researching more about design, my mentor began praising me for my improvements. This was a turning point for me as a designer and I developed a hunger to improve my craft.

I continued experimenting with Typography and incorporating a grid system for text-heavy pages. In addition, my mentor has a strong liking towards bold and blown up type. His infatuation rubbed off on me and I began incorporating blown up type to my designs.

Once I completed the magazine layout, I looked back at the results and I could see each page improving as I gained more knowledge and skill.

Reflections

This project was a turning point for my design career. During the time I was making this magazine, I was experiencing severe imposter syndrome and I felt that I was not fit to be in the design industry. Thankfully, my mentor at the time, gave me guidance and he pushed my limits as a designer. After producing the final product, I gained more confidence and I developed the hunger to continue improving my craft.
