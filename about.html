About me

I first discovered UI/UX while studying Graphic Design at the California State University, Fullerton. Since then, I’ve loved designing digital interfaces and now that I’m pursuing my undying love for video games, I’m eager to convey complex systems design while producing interfaces that align with a game’s art direction.

My goal is to work on diverse UI projects and collaborate with studios who welcome diversity and share a burning passion for video games.

Outside of work, I’m frequently playing through my Steam library, battling the finest bosses in the Souls-like games I own, maxing out in my mobile Anime gachas, and visiting every coffee and bubble milk tea shop in California.
