ABOUT ME
I’m a self-driven innovator focused on designing strategic user experiences and functional high-fidelity prototypes for web, mobile and XR products. I have developed an eye for visual design from my background in Graphic Design and it’s exciting to turn data into visual solutions.

2020 allowed me to greatly improve my design-thinking skills and to discover how I can truly be of service as an experience designer. Although I have experienced AR/VR through Pokemon Go and using a friend’s Oculus Quest, I never saw the beauty of immersive experiences until I recently bought my own VR headset and sat down and immersed myself in digital realities. I was captivated. Honestly, I regret not realizing this love for digital realities sooner.

I’m greatly interested in designing XR for healthcare, educational training, gaming and for the people.
