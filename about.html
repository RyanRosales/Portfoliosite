Background

I am currently a student attending the California State University, Fullerton and I am working towards a Bachelor’s Degree in Graphic and Interactive Design.

From October 2019 to January 2020, I was a Marketing Design intern for Infinite Peripherals, in Irvine, California. I led the visual design directions for Infinite Peripheral's product pages and translated my designs into coding language to achieve effective communication with the development team. In addition, I used my skills in Graphic Design and Typography to produce social media and print content; as well as assisting the Creative Director with the production of product motion graphics. Finally, I was given the opportunity to participate in Infinite Peripheral's vsual marketing for NRF 2020.

Working for Infinite Peripherals has granted me in-house opportunities to grow my design skills and learn business and marketing values. Overall, my hunger for knowledge and my desire to grow as an individual allowed me to adapt in a high-pressure environment from completing multiple projects on a time crunch and handling last-minute changes.

Whether I freelance or work full-time, I want to use my skills to bring my client's visions to life and to show the beauty of design.

Hit me up if you need a designer who will bring your vision to life or let's chat over virtual coffee and tea!
