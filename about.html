About me

My name is Ryan and I’m currently residing in sunny California. I’ve been a gamer all my life ever since I started playing Mario games on the Nintendo 64 and Gamecube. When I hit my college years, I dedicated too much of my time playing games related to eSports, in particular League of Legends and CSGO.

I attended the California State University, Fullerton to study Graphic Design. Right before the pandemic, I became very interested in UI/UX and once lockdowns took place, I dedicated my time learning UI/UX and getting a UX certificate, all while working on contract web design jobs.

I was trying to find my place in UI/UX across various industries, but I just couldn’t connect to one. Not one industry made my eyes light up the same way video games did. Then it dawned on me that I should’ve pursued games this whole time.

Motivated and obsessed with games now more than ever, I’ve been polishing my UI Design expertise and creating personal projects geared towards games. I’m currently seeking my first role as a UI Artist to help game studios accomplish their mission to produce new and exciting gaming experiences!

Ryan Rosales | Video Game UI/UX Designer
