Background

I am currently attending my last semester at the California State University, Fullerton. My expected graduation date is May 2020, with a Bachelor’s Degree in Graphic and Interactive Design.

In October 2019, I interned for Infinite Peripherals, in Irvine California. For the next three months, I worked under the marketing team with an emphasis on web design. I was given responsibility for the design direction of various product pages and I worked closely with the development team to produce the final results. In addition, I designed social media content for the company, assisted the Creative Director with editing product motion graphics and the development of UI screens for Infinite Peripherals’ NRF 2020 tradeshow.

I am obsessed with my craft and I actively research and cultivate my design skills daily. I try to make it a habit of thinking “UX” in my daily life and I express it with any person I interact with. I believe this habit reflects my desire in creating meaningful relationships through the interactions I come across.
