Hi! I’m Ryan Rosales. I am a Junior Product Designer based in Orange County, California who is seeking opportunities to forward a deeper connection between people and technology.

Background

I am currently attending my last semester at the California State University, Fullerton. My expected graduation date is May 2020, with a Bachelor’s Degree in Graphic and Interactive Design.

My portfolio reflects what I am currently cultivating my skills in:

• User Research
• Visual and Interaction design.

As a designer, it is a given that I strive to make things look strategically appealing, but I am all about the process. I can never complete a design project without researching and following the necessary steps that lead to the design. If I attempt to skip the process, I will sit in my chair, staring at my screen for hours, thinking about what to do. Even if I do get the "genius" idea, I still need to have a way that builds up to the final design. There is a beauty when gathering the puzzle pieces so that it results in a beautiful, yet effective piece of work.

Me on a Personal Level

Two things that people will most likely learn about me is that I am very enthusiastic about music and Japanese Anime. When I'm not tackling a creative project, my hobbies include singing, cosplaying and gaming. These hobbies of mine lets me really open up and be me.

If you would like to collab on a project or get to know me, hit me up! I’m always down to meet up and chat over coffee and tea.
