Full-Stack Developer Portfolio Website

A Freelance project where I designed a developer's portfolio

Project Overview

I took on a Freelance project mid March, 2020 where I worked on designing a Full-Stack Developer's Portfolio Wesbite. My client, Derek, and I set up a phone call to get to know each other and discuss the scope of work. I took the time to understand his personality and his goal for the project. He addressed that he wanted to create a portfolio to showcase his previous work as a Backend Developer and his Front-end development skills. In addition, he wanted his about page to share his life and hobbies, such as travel pictures and a feature that shares his Spotify playlists. After our phone call, we both signed a contract to settle our agreements for the project.

For the next two weeks, I worked on Derek's portfolio, frequently updating him on my progress on the project. I would apply the feedback he gave me after each iteration before the deadline. Once the deadline came along, he filled out the invoice and I sent him all the files stated in the contract.

It was a great pleasure working with Derek and I had a lot of fun, especially designing his about page. This was a new space for me to work in as it gave me the opportunity to challenge myself as a creative because I had to design features that reflected Derek's life and personality, based on my understanding of who he is as a person. Finally, he was gracious to connect me with a design studio in the Seattle area.
