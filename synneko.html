ENTER SYNNEKO
Our goal for the Synneko IoT app was themed around a friendly AI who is capable of understanding various human utterances and speech. This would eliminate voice commands, resulting in casual human-to-AI conversations. Your next best friend is an AI. Pretty dope right?

I was a Product Designer and along with a team of researchers and UX designers, we set out to produce a new smart speaker device for the current market.

I was involved in all stages of product design.

• Led the visual design
• Assisted in research studies and branding
• Prototyped
• Usability Testing


Timeframe: September, 2020 - October, 2020

Problem

UNDERSTANDING EXISTING SMART SPEAKER AND IOT DEVICES
Our first step was to expand our knowledge on smart speakers. We also researched various companies such as Google and Sonos to understand their design practices for IoT development. When we moved onto user interviews, we found a common problem among smart speaker users.
WHAT ARE THE EXISTING PROBLEMS?
We interviewed users across speaker brands such as Google and Amazon and they expressed concern toward their privacy and interacting with the AI system. Users were worried that the devices were passively “listening” to household conversations and collecting private information. In addition, voice AIs required very specific commands to function properly.
THE SOLUTIONS
An IoT onboarding experience that guarantees confidentiality of private information and an AI capable of engaging in casual human conversations.

Adding a Terms and Policies into the onboarding would help the product gain trust from users by only collecting information to better improve future updates and devices and will not be sold to or utilized by any third-party.

Since the AI, IRIS (Intelligent. Rad. Interactive. System.), can decipher various utterances and speech, this would not bind her to voice commands, resulting in a conversational and responsive virtual best friend.
PERSONAS
Our personas were centered around general necessities such as music and clear speaker quality, with the assurance that their personal and household information are safe.

BRANDING STYLE GUIDE
DESIGN SOLUTIONS
Within the onboarding experience, users will encounter two features that would guarantee the safety of their private information: Smart ID and Terms and Policies.

Smart ID enables users to log-in to the app via their voice; which would help eliminate identity theft.

Our Terms and Policies clearly states that information and data would be stored in a proprietary cloud system, used only to improve future devices, and would not be leaked to any third-party.
A NEW ARTIFICIAL EXISTENCE
Interacting with IRIS, the advanced AI is able to understand various utterances and speech.

This allows users to engage in casual human conversations with her and she can provide legitimate answers to even the most vague, poorly-worded questions.
