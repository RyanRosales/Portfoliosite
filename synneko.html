UX Case Study

SYNNEKO

End-to-end mobile IoT app

Overview

Our goals for the IoT design was to create an onboarding experience that reassures the safety of users' private information and a voice UI that encourages casual conversations between humans and AI.

The Problem

More households are adopting smart speaker devices to make daily living easier. However, are these devices completely harmless or do they pose a more sinister threat to privacy?

  • Project: IterateUX Design Challenge

  • Timeline: 3 weeks, October, 2020

  • Role: Ideation, wireframes, prototype

  • Software: Figma, Miro

RESEARCH

Our first step was to expand our knowledge on smart speakers. We researched companies such as Google and Sonos to understand their design practices for IoT development.



While we expanded our knowledge, we interviewed 5 smart speaker users. Feedback we received from our users across brands such as Google and Amazon, expressed their concern toward their privacy and voice interactions with the AI systems. They were worried that the devices were passively “listening” to household conversations and collecting private information. In addition, voice AIs require specific commands to function properly; which makes them incapable of answering vague requests.
