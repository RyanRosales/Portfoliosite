30 Days of UI Interactions

A Daily UI Design and Animation Challenge

Overview

I took on a 30 day design project in order to hone my skills in UX. Participants were given creative freedom so I decided to work on UI interactions on Adobe After Effects.

My plan was to design specific UI themes in 10 day intervals. The first 5 days were practicing specific interactions in basic form and then the remaining 5 days I advanced to full app screen interactions.

Process

I researched various UI interactions to see what kind of animations I was interested in and what I felt I could accomplish during the project. After the research process, I compiled a list of themes I wanted to do for each 10 day intervals. I began sketching out how I wanted each animation to be. If I had time, I would plan out future animation ideas ahead of time, otherwise I would do the planning process the day of each animation.

When I felt satisfied with each idea, I worked on Adobe XD to develop the wireframes. If there were vector illustrations involved, I would work on Adobe Illustrator first then import it to XD.

Once the UI designs were completed, I imported them into Adobe After Effects. From here I would try to limit my time on animating to 2-3 hours.

Once satisfied with the result, I would export my animations as mp4 files and then upload them onto Instagram.

Objectives

• Improve my animation skills on After Effects.

• Gain a better understanding on UI interactions.

• Gain a faster workflow.

Challenges

• Budgeting my time with this project, 5 classes and my internship.

• Upload work onto Instagram before each midnight.

• Limit myself to 2-3 hours of working on this project each day.

• Don't give up

Format

• Day 1-5: Button interactions

• Day 5-10: Full screen button interactions

• Days 10-15: Jelly Animations & Parallax

• Days 15-20:Full screen Jelly Animations & Parallax

• Days 20-25: Full screen interactions

• Days 25-30: Full screen interactions

Reflections

I stayed true to the 30 day project and uploaded each day. This project was indeed a challenge since I had to manage my time properly each day. Overall, I enjoyed doing this project and I have gained so much knowledge in After Effects and UI interactions.

On a final note, I believe it's necessary for UX designers to understand how interactions work, especially when collaborating with developers.
