eSports Magazine

A handcrafted magazine that highlights the competitive gaming culture.

Overview

A design challenge I took on during spring 2018 that required original design and content, along with handcrafting the final product. In addition, I was given the freedom to choose any topic I wanted to cover. As an avid gamer, I wanted to cover the trending eSports culture and highlight the environment gamers live in.

A Little Background Story

During the process of designing the layout of the magazine, I struggled because I was still understanding Graphic Design had no prior knowledge of magazine design. As I kept working on the layout and researching more about design, my mentor saw my slow improvements.

I continued experimenting with Typography and incorporated a grid system for text-heavy pages. In addition, my mentor had a strong liking towards bold and blown up type. His infatuation rubbed off on me and I began incorporating blown up type to my designs.

This project was a turning point for my design career. During the time I was making this magazine, I was experiencing severe imposter syndrome and I felt that I was not fit to be in the design industry. Thankfully, my mentor at the time, saw my potential and he pushed my limits as a designer. After producing the final product, I gained more confidence and I developed the hunger to continue improving my craft.
